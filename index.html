What is learner experience design? |

LX is a learner-centric approach that focuses on the ENTIRE learning journey, not just what happens in the classroom. And while technologies change rapidly, the underlying principles of adult learning do not. Behind successful programs are proven educational theories that, when applied right, drive great results. It's time to take a fresh look at your program of instruction.

Life is not about finding yourself. Life is about creating yourself ...

So turn over a new leaf!

| Define |

Using 'Design Thinking' principles, I work with you to set learning objectives that tie into your business goals. This is the most important step of the process, but is often short-changed.

| Design |

Design is an essential component to achieving those specific goals. Regardless of the chosen technology, great design merges seamlessly with your business goals and learning objectives.

| Deliver |

Once you are equipped with an elegantly designed solution to your specific problem, it is time for delivery! I work with you to deliver the program (through technology, face-to-face, role-playing), and help you measure results and refine.
